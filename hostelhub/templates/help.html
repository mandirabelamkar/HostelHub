Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my hostel fees?

Go to the Fees section and click on 'Pay Now' for any pending records. We support various payment methods.

What happens if my complaint is rejected?

In case of rejection, please review the 'Resolution Remarks' provided by the admin for the reason and further steps.

Can I change my room mid-semester?

Room changes are subject to availability and administration approval. Please visit the admin office for inquiries.
